Gameplay Rules (Most Points Wins)

Pick Em' Rules
Predict if the character lives or dies

Main Characters:
Predict Dies: 15pts
Predict Lives: 10pts
Secondary Characters:
Predict Dies: 10pts
Predict Lives: 5pts

Predict if the character becomes a wight walker after death

This is optional
Correct guesses: 4pts
Incorrect guesses: -4pts

Predict which episode character dies

Correct guesses: 8pts

Predict who the character is killed by (non-wights)

If you believe the character will be killed by another character and not a Wight, predict who you think it will be
Correct guesses: 15pts

Sure Thing Multiplier
Choose 5 characters that are "Sure Things"

If your prediction for that character is correct, the score gained for that character is multiplied by 1.5

Ex: You correctly predict it's a "Sure Thing" Jon Snow is gonna live:
10pts * 1.5 = 15pts gained

Predict the Outcome Bonuses
Gain points for answering the bonus questions correctly

Each question will have a corresponding point total
